Protocol Guild is a collective funding mechanism for Ethereum's core protocol contributors. It has received over $92.9M in pledges and donations from the Ethereum ecosystem and currently distributes funds to 187+ active Ethereum core developers. Major contributors include Lido, Uniswap, ENS, and many others.
