The Ecosystem Support Program (ESP) provides grants to teams and individuals working on projects that benefit the Ethereum ecosystem. In 2024, ESP allocated approximately $3M across 105 projects covering research, development, community building, and education. The program supports work on core protocol, Layer 2, security, and ecosystem tooling.
