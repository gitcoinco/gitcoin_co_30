## Overview

Aerodrome Finance is the leading decentralized exchange (DEX) and governance hub on Base. It combines automated market making (AMM) with vote‑escrowed tokenomics (veNFT) to align liquidity provision with protocol governance. For Nigerian DeFi users, Aerodrome offers:

- **Low‑gas swaps** on Base (fraction of a cent vs. Ethereum mainnet)
- **Real yield** from trading fees and bribe markets
- **Governance participation** via locked AERO tokens (veNFT)
- **Airdrop potential** for active users and liquidity providers

This guide distills the essential mechanics and provides a Naija‑focused playbook for getting started profitably.

## Why Aerodrome Matters for Nigeria

- **Cost efficiency** – Base’s low fees make frequent trading viable even with small capital.
- **Yield opportunities** – Nigerians can earn passive income providing liquidity or farming bribes without expensive mining rigs.
- **On‑ramp simplicity** – Many Nigerian fiat on‑ramps (e.g., Quidax, Dart Africa) support bridging to Base via Multichain or LayerZero.
- **Community growth** – Aerodrome’s governance allows Nigerian DAOs to influence tokenomics and fee structures.

## Quick‑Start: Connect, Swap, Earn

### 1. Add Base to Your Wallet

- **MetaMask/Coinbase Wallet:**  
  RPC: `https://mainnet.base.org`  
  Chain ID: `8453`  
  Symbol: `ETH`

### 2. Bridge Funds to Base

- **Official bridge**: https://bridge.base.org (from Ethereum mainnet)
- **Third‑party bridges**: Multichain, LayerZero, Hop Protocol (often cheaper/faster)
- **Nigerian on‑ramps**: Quidax, Dart Africa, Busha – buy crypto Naira and withdraw to Base via bridge partners.

### 3. Approve and Swap

- Go to https://aerodrome.finance
- Connect wallet (Base network)
- Select tokens (e.g., ETH, USDC, AERO)
- Set slippage (0.5–1% recommended)
- Confirm swap; pay gas in ETH.

### 4. Provide Liquidity

- Navigate to “Pools”
- Choose a pool (high volume: AERO/USDC, ETH/USDC)
- Add equal value of both tokens
- Receive LP tokens; stake them in “Gauge” to earn AERO + fees.

### 5. Vote on Gauges (veNFT)

- Lock AERO tokens (1‑4 years) to receive voteweight (veNFT).
- Vote weekly on which pools receive AERO emissions.
- Bribes: protocols offer additional tokens to attract your vote; claim them directly.

## Naija‑Specific Strategies

- **Gas‑savvy trading** – batch swaps during low‑traffic hours (UTC off‑peak).
- **Yield stacking** – combine Aerodrome LP positions with Base quests (e.g., DeFiLlama yield aggregator) for extra rewards.
- **Peer‑to‑peer (P2P) hedging** – Nigerians can use stablecoins (USDC) on Aerodrome to hedge Naira volatility without leaving the chain.
- **Community DAO** – pool veNFT voting power among a syndicate of Nigerian traders to influence gauge allocations and collect larger bribes.

## Risks & Considerations

- **Impermanent loss** – liquidity providers can lose value if token prices diverge.
- **Smart contract risk** – while audited, always test small amounts first.
- **Regulatory uncertainty** – Nigerian SEC guidelines on DeFi remain unclear; use at own discretion.
- **Bridge risks** – cross‑chain bridges have been exploited; prefer Base’s official bridge or well‑audited partners.
